Capricorn Traits

Symbol:The Glyph (Sea-Goat)

Element:Earth

Quality:Cardinal

Keyword:"I Use"

Ruling Planet:Saturn

Strengths:Determination, Strong willed, Disciplined, Responsible

Weakness:Unforgiving, Pessimistic

People under the sign of Capricorn are often serious minded. They have a sense of inner freedom and masters of self-control which gives them the ability to lead people, make big plans and work on them. They are able to climb up the ladder of success due to this self discipline and dedication. Smart, hardworking, and fully in control of their destiny, a Capricorn will always get what they set their mind to, in both personal and professional life. Capricorns may get a reputation as stubborn, but they simply know what they want.

Natural rule-followers, Capricorns thrive on order and love strict rules, hierarchies, and set ways to do things. Capricorns are great at climbing the corporate ladder, and especially adept at making (and investing) money. Capricorns love family, and put a lot of stock in traditions. A Capricorn might stress themselves out trying to do everything perfectly. They may feel like they must present a home-cooked meal every day, and would never admit to doing drive-thru or takeout. Capricorns believe presentation is everything, and their homes are usually Insta-worthy. A Capricorn sometimes is overly focused on what things look like, instead of how things feel, which could cause them to feel stifled and unhappy.

They make loyal friends and have a funny and sly sense of humour. Often they turn out to be the” Mom friend” in their friend circle due to their caring nature just like their opposite sign, Cancer.



Capricorns can be incredibly hard on themselves, and just as hard on other people. They can hold grudges and hold other people in their lives to impossible standards. Capricorns sometimes see one narrow way of doing something, and broadening their horizons and perspective can be incredibly helpful in giving them and others a break.
