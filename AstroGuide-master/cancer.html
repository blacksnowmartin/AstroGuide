Cancer Traits

Symbol:The Crab

Element:Water

Quality:Cardinal

Keyword:"I Feel"

Ruling Planet:Moon

Strengths:Empathy, affectionate, Home oriented, caring

Weakness:Clingy, insecure, pessimistic, Moody

Belonging to the group of “most nurturing signs” of the zodiac, Cancers tend to be maternal and caring towards people they love. They are caring and sensitive but they wont let anyone walk over them. They are emotionally intelligent individuals who can feel emotions and feeling of other people.



Ruled by the moon, they tend to be moody as their internal side and emotional patterns are mostly affected by the lunar cycle and an change in their moods will be observed during various lunar phases. They tend to be empathetic towards people and although they are soft hearted they have strong emotional sides. They have the strength to put up with a lot of emotional hurdles.

Like Taurus, Cancers are home oriented individuals and prefer staying within the comforts of their house rather than adventuring outside. They tend to make good housekeepers.

They are quick to help others in times of need and often avoid conflicts at all costs. However they will be the first person to stand up for their friends or family in case of conflicts. The crab should never be underestimated as they are one of the most dangerous zodiac signs. They can be the best person in your life showering you with love, affection and care but this can turn ugly if you hurt them.

Since cancers are sensitive individuals, they tend to be within their hard shells and wont come out of it unless they feel emotionally secure to do so. They have this natural ability to protect themselves just like the crab.
