Compatibility with Signs

Before we go on about how the signs go about with each other, let me tell you one thing. Astrology never says that a couple cannot be in love or in a relationship. Love exists between two individuals when they have a mutual feeling of love and respect for each other and the will to be there for their loved one no matter the circumstances. A strong relationship stands on the foundation of trust, loyalty and faith which is cemented by both partners. If you and your partner love each other to the core, then neither astrology nor the whole world can tear it apart.

Astrology only tells us how the signs connect with each other, how they handle problems together and make a perfect couple. Their compatibility can be roughly understood by the way the elements behave when they are brought together.

There are 4 elements in the zodiac- Fire, Earth, Air and Water. All the elements have 3 signs under them. Listing them down for you:

Fire: Aries, Leo, Sagittarius
Earth: Taurus, Virgo, Capricorn
Air: Gemini, Libra, Aquarius
Water: Cancer, Scorpio, Pisces

Now if we observe our daily life, we know how the elements co-exist with each other. Let’s observe a simple phenomenon. You take a mug full of mud or sand and pour it into a bowl. Next take a mug of water and pour it into the same bowl containing the mud. What do you observe? The water seeps into the mud completely and forms a wet mixture. If it was just mud and air, there would be no mixture (Well technically somewhere 1 or 2% of air would be within the mud). This shows how an earth sign ‘mixes’ with a water and air sign. Similarly, we all know this scientific fact that air is one of the three factors required for a fire to burn. But mud or water can extinguish it. In the same way fire signs are compatible more with air signs as compared to earth or water signs.
