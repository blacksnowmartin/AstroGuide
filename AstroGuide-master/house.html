Houses and its Significance

In astrology, the 12 houses are just like the 12 zodiac signs that create the foundation for your life. These houses reveal a more elucidated pattern of growth and development reflecting the 12 signs. It represents the areas of experience where the energies of the zodiac sign and planets function together. These are determined based on the position of planets at the time of birth. These 12 houses focus on enlightening one’s inner state of mind, experience, and temperaments.


The First House

Ruled By Aries

The First House is known as ‘Lagna’ or ‘Ascendant’. It is named as "House of Self". It’s the house which represents you, the way you look. Basically, your physical personality which includes physical appearance, temperament, nature, body frame, childhood, health, ego and their sense of self. It affects your life choices,sense of knowing your strengths, weaknesses. Along with your likes, dislikes and the way you might wish others to perceive through your opinions, attitude and viewpoints. The mains parts depicted by the 1st house are the head and the face.


The Second House

Ruled By Taurus

The Second House is often referred to as the "House of Possessions". The second house rules your core values and covers matters related to your material and physical world. It represents how well you manage your finances, how you value and earn money and your self-esteem. Using your possessions including your non-material things to the fullest also falls under the 2nd house’s realm. The body organs ruled by 2nd house include tongue, teeth, eyes, mouth, nose, facial bones, upper neck and bones etc.


The Third House

Ruled By Gemini

The Third House is also known as "House of Communications".It governs the mental inclination of a person and their ability to memorize. This house also relates to interests, habits, mental intelligence and communication.

It rules over different modes of communication, such as media, television, telephone, radio, writing, editing, etc. All sorts of professions related to communication are included in this dominion. The body parts third house rules include legs, hands, arms, shoulders, collar bones, lungs, and nervous system.


The Fourth House

Ruled By Cancer

The Fourth House is referred to as "House of Home and Family".The fourth house rules your houses as well as your roots, land, real estate matters, vehicles etc. It also defines your relation with your mother. This house is associated with the domestic happiness of a person. The body organs governed by the fourth house are stomach, breasts and digestive organs.


The Fifth House

Ruled By Leo

The Fifth House is the "House of Creativity, Playfulness, Pleasure, and Romance". It signifies your mental intelligence, your capability to create and innovate. This house also relates to fortune, good luck, learning, and optimism. The body parts that the fifth house rules over include heart, upper and middle back, stomach, pancreas, and spine.


The Sixth House

Ruled By Virgo

The Sixth House is the "House of Health".This house corresponds mainly to your health, wellness, and your daily life routine. It represents the choices that you make on a daily basis. This house also relates to debts, obstacles, enemies, and difficulties. This house rules the body parts like waist, lower abdomen, kidney, navel, small intestine, the upper part of the large intestine, appendix, etc.



The Seventh House

Ruled By Libra

The Seventh House is often referred to as the "House of Marriage".The Seventh House, also known as the descendent, sits directly across from the first house (Ascendant). It’s the house of your spouse/partner. It is also the house of all kinds of partnerships including business partnership and also deals with the darker side of your relationships, the partnerships you form. Planets moving through the Seventh House help you close deals, securing the bonds by signing contracts and making things official. It governs various body parts including kidneys, ovaries and the lower half of the back.


The Eighth House

Ruled By Scorpio

The Eighth House is also called "House of Reincarnation".The Eighth House rules over death, longevity and sudden events like Lottery. It is also related to wealth. This house is also the house of mysteries and transformations. It can bring depression, miseries, lack of mental peace, imprisonment etc to the person affected. However, on a positive note, the native may have strong intuitive skills and such people can also master the fields of psychology, astrology, mathematics, and paranormal activities. Several various body parts governed by this sign include pelvic bones and external sexual organs.


The Ninth House

Ruled By Sagittarius

Truth, principles, dreams, and intuitions all define the ninth house. It deals with your religious instincts, immigration, good karma, dharma, ethics, higher learnings, one’s inclination towards good deeds and charity. The Ninth House corresponds with Sagittarius energy which is signified by Jupiter that makes this house the "House of Luck, Fortune, and Favours". The various parts of the body under control of this house are thighs, thigh bones, bone marrow, left leg, and arterial system.


The Tenth House

Ruled By Capricorn

The Tenth House is also called "House of Career and Social Status".Also known as The Medium Coeli , the tenth house is at the very top and also considered to be the most public on the zodiac wheel. This house deals with the kind of work you do, the profession you are in, your prestige, your reputation, etc. your area of occupation is defined by the planetary positions in this house. This house corresponds with the Capricorn energy and ruled by Saturn. This house represents your father and the way he has influenced you throughout your childhood. Different body parts ruled by this house are knees, kneecaps, bones, and joints.


The Eleventh House

Ruled By Aquarius

The Eleventh House is also called "House of Prosperity". It’s a strong indicator of wealth and income, gain in name, fame, and money, and also plays a role in determining what brings you profits. Your identity within a group is identified by the Eleventh House. Social circle, friends, groups, memberships in organizations and societies are ruled by this house. If you have too many planets in this house then you will have a large group of friends and you need them every time.


The Twelfth House

Ruled By Pisces

Being the last house,the Twelfth House represents the ending of your life cycle and the beginning of your spiritual journeys. It is also called the "House of Unconscious, Self- Undoing, and Imprisonment". A weak Twelfth House may give the native detachment from people like separation from their partner, parents, friends, and neighbours. The detachment can also be in the form of death. Mostly it governs the intangible things. Things like intuitions, dreams, secrets, and emotions because this house corresponds with Pisces energy and is ruled by Neptune planet. The twelfth house takes care of the body organs like the left eye, feet, and lymphatic system.



Planets and their Impact

Sun:The Ego

The sun shifts into a new sign every month, and as a personal planet, it represents the ego, the inner self, willpower, and lifeforce. The sun represents the broad strokes of the personality, the essence inside of us that spurs us to action.


Moon:The Emotions

The moon moves into a new sign every two to three days, and represents the emotional nature of the sign it is in. The moon governs our subconscious, our emotional reactions to new circumstances, the part of ourselves that is purely emotional and instinctual, our relationships with our mothers, and our childhood wounds.


Mercury:The Communications

Mercury moves into a new sign every three to four weeks, and is the planet that rules our rational mind, our capacity to collect and sort out information and to pass it on to others. It has power over our written and spoken word and our curiosity.


Venus:Love and Beauty

Venus moves signs every four to five weeks. It is known to be the planet of love, beauty, and money.In relationships, Venus represents what we desire from our partners in order to feel loved. Venus also rules over the kinds of things we spend money on, what we consider beautiful, the arts, good food, and creature comforts.


Mars: Conflict and Aggression

Mars is about initiative, sexual drive, and passion. In relationships, Mars is the planet that governs our primal sexual instincts, what we bring to a relationship, and how we go after what we want.Mars is the heated passion behind our actions, and shifts into a new sign every six to seven weeks, changing the way we take initiative.



Jupiter: Luck, growth and wisdom

Jupiter is the planet of expansion, foreign travel, and the search for meaning and truth. It represents philosophy, religion, metaphysics, and education. Jupiter is the planet that pushes us to continue exploring, asking questions, and look for answers. It's also the planet that can lead us into overindulgence. Jupiter moves into a new sign every two to three years.


Saturn: Discipline and Challenges

Saturn is known as the great disciplinarian planet. It is the planet of rules, restriction, personal responsibility, respect for tradition, and the courage to face our fears. Saturn is the laws, boundaries, and limits set by society. It shapes our sense of personal duty during times of hardship. It moves into a new sign every two to three years, taking its sweet time with each sign to teach its hard-fought lessons.


Uranus: Innovation and Change

Uranus governs invention, new ways of thinking, revolutionary ideas. Uranus's energy is usually described as sudden, shocking, and disruptive. It represents our urge to stand out as true individuals, and it is the planet that will force changes on a dime when the old ways of thinking stop working for us. It takes a full seven years to move from one sign into another, defining the ideas of entire generations of people each time it moves into a new sign.


Neptune: Dreams and Healing

Neptune is the planet that governs the urge to dissolve the ego, to become one with others, and to enter into altered states of being. Neptune rules our dreams and ideals; it represents things that aren't quite as they appear, the illusive, and the unreal. It is the planet of receptivity, imagination, cloudiness, confusion, delusion, illusion, and unreality. Neptune moves into a new sign every 10 to 12 years influences our urge for spiritual healing, but at its worst can lead us toward escapism through the use of mind-altering drugs.


Pluto: Death and Transformation

Pluto governs death and rebirth. This powerful planet has dominion over drastic changes that bring true upheavals. It tears things down to build them back up. Pluto will transform the areas of your life that need to undergo regeneration, whether you asked for it or  not. Every time you experience dramatic crises or have to endure terrible suffering for the purpose of healing, it can be linked to Pluto's movement through your chart. Being such an unpredictable planet, Pluto moves into a new sign every 12 to 15 years.
